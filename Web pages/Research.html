Products Blog Research About Us

For the majority our home page is an original design. But the idea of an image slider was taken from other website called W3 schools. The code for the image slider was taken from the site however it was edited and formatted accroding to our needs.

For our Blog page, the inspiration to use both pictures and videos to make our blog more informative and creative in a way was taken by studying the different blogs and their formats in the Pluralsight website.

On our products page, the plants are aligned into their own respective box as cards. This way of displaying the plants was an inspiration from the W3 schools website. The website provides the code for doing so and we took the code and modified it for use on our page.


While researching for a good design for about us page, we stumbled upon the big cartel website. This website helps people to sell their art. After looking at the card format on the about page, we immediately went with this idea of displaying invovled members. As you can see on the left and on the right, the members are displayed on a card format.
