Akadama Medium Grain

Different sizes of Akadama soil are perfect for those looking to use in different size pots or to mix of other materials. Akadama is one of the most popular materials used for soil for bonsai trees. Akadama is a fired clay that retains its form when dry and absorbs water when wet. This provides the tree with moisture while preventing compaction. Akadama provides excellent drainage and aeration for healthy root growth. It's an ideal ingredient for every soil mix and works with all bonsai trees.
